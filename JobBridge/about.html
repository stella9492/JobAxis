About Us

Welcome to JobAxis Kenya! Your premier recruitment portal designed to connect talented individuals with their dream jobs in Kenya and beyond. At JobAxis, we believe in empowering job seekers and employers alike by bridging the gap in the job market.

Our platform is dedicated to not only listing jobs but also providing comprehensive resources for career advancement. We offer valuable insights into creating standout CVs and cover letters, ensuring our users are well-prepared to seize opportunities.

For Employers, JobAxis acts as a powerful recruitment tool, simplifying the hiring process. With our user-friendly platform, you can effortlessly post job openings, access a rich database of qualified candidates, and find the perfect match for your team. Our services include online job advertisements, tailored recruitment solutions, and expert training in CV and cover letter writing.

For Job Seekers, JobAxis provides open access to a wide array of job listings and essential resources for career growth. Whether you’re a fresh graduate or an experienced professional, our goal is to support your journey towards success.

Our Vision

To be the leading job portal in Africa, recognized globally for our innovative approach and exceptional service.

Our Mission

To be the first choice for job seekers and employers, facilitating seamless connections and empowering individuals to achieve their career aspirations.
